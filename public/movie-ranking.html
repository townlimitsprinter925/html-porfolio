Travis' Favorite Movies

Here is a non-comprehensive list of movies I've enjoyed.

50/50

This movie is about a 30-something who struggles dealing emotionally and socially with a diagnosis of an advanced, nonoperative cancer. It's a heavy, but relatable story of humanity.

Definitely, maybe

I'm a sucker for a cheesy rom-com. Ryan Gosling and the girl from Little Miss Sunshine, plus Isla Fisher, made for a fantastic storyline.

007: Spectre

I really enjoy Daniel Craig as James Bond. He's fit, suave and incredibly precise as a double-agent.
